For most of the experiment, you will play a simple game called VestiTito, which is a game played in groups of five players. The goal of the game is to correctly guess the colors of the clothes of a mysterious character. There are five pieces of clothing that will appear grey at the start of the game and need to be colored. The payment reward depends on the number of colors correctly guessed by you and the other members of your group. In order to guess the colors you will need to rely on a hint you will receive and on the communication with the other members of the group. Each player will indeed receive a different piece of information regarding one of the colors. In total there are five colors that you can try to guess and five pieces of information will be distributed among the members of the group. Remember that if you are not confident regarding one of the colors you can leave it grey. The total score is obtained by adding up two components:

  • A preliminary score that depends on the number of colors correctly guessed and on the number of mistakes.
  • A bonus given to the player with the highest preliminary score (or shared among the players with the highest preliminary score).

Specifically, the preliminary score will be calculated as follows:

  • You will receive 10 tokens for every color correctly guessed.
  • You will lose 5 tokens for every wrong guess.
  • You will receive 0 tokens for every piece of clothing left grey.

The bonus consists in a total of 100 tokens, distributed among the players in the group that got the highest preliminary score:

  • You will receive a bonus of 100 tokens if your preliminary score will be higher than the score of any other member of your group.
  • You will receive a bonus of 50 tokens in case your preliminary score is the same as the one obtained by one other member of the group and higher than the scores obtained by the rest of the group.
  • You will receive a bonus of 33 tokens in case your preliminary score is the same as the one obtained by two other members of the group and higher than the scores obtained by the rest of the group.
  • You will receive a bonus of 25 tokens in case your preliminary score is the same as the one obtained by three other members of the group and higher than the score obtained by the last member of the group.
  • You will receive a bonus of 20 tokens in case your preliminary score is the same as the score of any other member of the group.
  • You will receive a bonus of 0 tokens in case there is another member of the group with a preliminary score that is higher than yours.

On the top of the page you will be able to see two pieces of information. The first one is a hint regarding one of the five colors you need to guess. For example, if you see “The color of the Hat is Red” you know for sure that the right guess for the hat is the color red. The second piece of information concerns the group of people you will be playing with. You will see a picture representing a network. In the network, each node (A, B, C, D, and E) represents a member of the group. Moreover, two nodes are connected by a link if they can chat during the round. For example, you could receive the following message:

The following network structure was randomly selected for your group and you have been assigned to node A.

This will tell you the following:

  • Your position in the network is A, therefore you will be able to chat with player B.
  • Player B will be able to chat with you and with player E.
  • Player C will be able to chat with player D and player E.
  • Player D will be able to chat with player C and player E.
  • Player E will be able to chat with player B, player C, and player D.

The one above is just an example, you may be assigned to a different position to a network with different links as the ones in the picture.

Below the information regarding the network you will see the mysterious character. Initially all clothes will be colored grey. It is possible to change color by clicking on the piece of clothing and then selecting the new choice. You will be able to change colors as many times as you wish during the round, this will not affect your score. The only combination of colors that will be used to calculate the score is the last combination selected at the end of the round.

The bottom of the page contains the chat. You will be able to communicate with other players according to the network, as described above. If you want, you can use the chat to exchange information with other players concerning the colors. You are free to write the messages you prefer, and this will not affect your score. The only limitations are about the language and about anonymity. Please write your messages in English and make sure you do not reveal anything about your identity during the game.

The same game will be played four times in rounds of 10 minutes each and with different groups of players, so that you won’t be interacting more than once with the same person. Your payoffs for each round will be added up and will contribute to your final earnings at the end of the experiment.
