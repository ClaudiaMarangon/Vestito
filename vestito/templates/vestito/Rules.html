In this game you will play against 4 other players:

  1. You and your 4 competitors will be randomly assigned to a mysterious character. You have to guess the colors of the clothes of the character you are assigned to.
  2. You will receive an hint about the color of one of the clothes of your mysterious character.
  3. You will receive some information on one or more players of your group.
  4. Careful! You can see the hint just once!
  5. You have 15 minutes to change your choices about the colors. During this time you can communicate with your competitors, if you want to.
  6. You will gain 10 points for each correct color, you will lose 5 points for each color you guess wrong and 0 points for each piece of clothing that you leave grey.
  7. There is a bonus of 100 points which will be divided among the members of the group who will have guessed correctly the highest number of colors. That is, you will win:
    • 100 points if all the other participants will have correctly guessed less colors than you
    • 50 points if just another player will have correctly guess as many colors as you, and no one will have guessed more
    • 33 points if two more players will have correctly guess as many colors as you, and no one will have guessed more
    • 25 points if three more players will have correctly guess as many colors as you, and no one will have guessed more
    • 20 points if you have correctly guessed the same number of colors as the other participants
  8. At the end of each round you will be asked to answer two questions about that round.
  9. You will play a total number of three rounds.
  10. The game will end at the end of the third round. One of the three rounds will be randomly selected to calculate the final payoff. You will get 10 cents for each point gained in the extracted round.
